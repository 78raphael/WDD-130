Overview

Purpose

Hi, I’m a Web Developer. I create clean and structured websites that promote your business. A well-designed site not only provides your potential customers with valuable information but also can create a brand that helps identify you among the sea of websites. A well designed internet site can keep your existing customers up to date on all your latest info but also attract new visitors to find out more about you and your business. Think about one of your favorite, non-social media, websites. What draws you there? The design? The content? The owner? Think if we could get these same things working for you. Wouldn’t it be worth investing in yourself and your business to be the owner of a site like this?

Audience

My intended audience are those small business owners who want to get started with putting their business online and seek new customers. First time business owners who are not sure where to start or how to get online but know that they need to put their company or product online and not just one a social media marketplace or advertise on social media only. Ages from 19 and up who have a service or product they want to reach a larger audience and also have a more professional appearance and feel than just social media.

Content

Home page

My intended audience would be people seeking services of a web developer to build them a site for their business. Mainly targeting small business owners or people who are not sure where to start when trying to launch their idea for a business venture. There are people who are trying to get their idea off the ground and are searching for a way to put their products or services out on the internet in a more professional manner than just creating a social media page and posting pictures. While this may be part of what is incorporated into their website, this is not the only way to out themselves ‘out there’. The social media platforms can reach a wide array of people with varying interest and curiosities. However, most social media platforms are not equipped to provide all the necessary material to potential customers to inform them and display the full range of goods and services that a business owner offers. Also, keeping current customers apprised of new products and services is also necessary and can get lost on a social media platform. Social media can provide an introduction and a preview, but there needs to be a place customers can go to get the full spectrum offered items and their descriptions. This is where my website comes in. This website will be the place where these small business owners can come to see what web developer services are offered. They can get a sense of the types of services and maybe pricing.

The homepage would display some contact info, so that doesn’t need it’s own page. There would also be a bit of description, most likely a summary or a variant of what was stated in the previous section of this page. Images might show some technical, designed images, maybe a couple of office type people with a computer displayed. Maybe even a scene from nature to provide a bit of contrast to the technical imagery already displayed.
