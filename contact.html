Our Story

We started as a small business with a handful of kayaks we purchased from various sources online and those donated from friends and family. After making sure each vessel was safe for use, we began taking out small groups of friends, family, and those in our town on small excursions. What was a fun hobby that we shared with those in our town, turned into a profitable business startup.

Our first few years, we relied on the donated and 2nd-hand equipment. As our popularity and business grew, we were able to purchase new and specialized equipment and tools. After years of working on our own and others kayaks and related equipment, we became proficient at all the various models and styles of kayaks. We were doing what we loved and making a name for ourselves.

We began in an unused barn a few miles out of town. As we grew, we were able to move to a small building in town a few years later. Business boomed and we upgraded to our current location in a spacious warehourse that could house our entire business. Not bad for a couple high school friends who bonded over cutting class and riding the river.
